**Added:** Adjustable playback speed for editor clips.

Set individual timeline clips anywhere from 0.25x slow motion to 16x speed, with timing kept consistent throughout the editing workflow.

- **Editor controls:** Choose a clip speed from the compact timeline toolbar menu and preview the result immediately.
- **Timeline editing:** Trimming, splitting, clip placement, and recording bookmarks stay aligned with the selected speed.
- **Saving and sharing:** Saved edits, clipboard copies, and exported videos preserve the chosen speed, duration, and audio timing.
